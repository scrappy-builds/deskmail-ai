Design system v1

Dark, clean, technical.
Engineering-adjacent, never corporate.

This is the single source of truth for DeskMail AI's look and feel. When you add a feature — a new panel, a setting, a dialog — pull the tokens, type, and components from here so it lands as part of the same product, not a bolt-on. The brand signals precision and practicality: made by someone who knows what they're doing and is honest that they're still learning.

01

Colour

Near-black surfaces carry the interface; a single green — the logo cube colour — is the one point of colour and marks anything interactive. Grey does the quiet work. Claude's amber is the only warm accent, reserved for AI. Values below are the live theme; toggle to compare.

Typography
